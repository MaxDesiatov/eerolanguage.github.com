about eero

Removing the “slum of braces” from Objective-C

My wife once told me about the idea behind the design of the Tulip chair and Pedestal table by the Finnish-American architect and industrial designer Eero Saarinen. His goal in creating this table and chairs, which rest on round bases, was to clean up the “slum of legs” that had always been present in dining-room furniture. Given our mutual interest in reduction and simplification, it seemed fitting to name this dialect after him.

about me
